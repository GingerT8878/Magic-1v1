Field guide

How to play

Win the duel

Reduce the other fighter's health to zero. At the start, each side selects a class; every class has different base stats and a starting weapon, creating faster, tougher, weapon-focused, or magic-focused play styles.

Health
Reach zero and the duel is lost.
Stamina
Powers weapons and movement.
Mana
Powers magic spells.
Speed
Controls how far Approach, Step Back, Charge, and Escape move the fighter.
Distance
Controls weapon reach and spell accuracy.

Choose a play style

Before the duel, choose your fighter class. In 2-Player, each player chooses separately; in Singleplayer, choose your class and the opponent class or Random. Class stats stay fixed, while scavenging can replace the starting weapon and its bonus.

Weapons

Weapon range determines how close you must be to attack. Every equipped weapon also grants its listed stat bonus.

Combat actions

🚶‍➡️ApproachMove closer at no stamina cost.
🚶Step BackMove away by the same amount as Approach at no stamina cost.
⚔️AttackStrike with your equipped weapon.
🏃‍➡️ChargeMove at full speed, then attack if weapon range is reached. Assassin alone stuns and gains an extra action after a successful Charge; Swordsman Charge can critically hit.
🏃EscapeIncrease distance for 10 stamina.
🧘MeditateRandomly restores 0–5 health, stamina, and mana independently.
🔍ScavengeSpend a random 0–5 stamina and 0–5 mana to search for items or weapons.

Magic

Spells always land within their hit range. Between hit range and maximum range, accuracy decreases linearly to 0% at maximum range.

🔥Fireball · 25 manaDeals 15 immediate damage. Fire then deals 10 damage per turn for 2 turns, for 20 additional damage if both ticks complete. Hit range 4 · Max range 8.
🧊Freeze · 40 manaDeals 5 damage, encases the target in ice, and grants 3 different bonus actions. Charge and Freeze may be combined, but each can only be used once during the whole turn. Active class abilities can consume a bonus action. Hit range 5 · Max range 10.
❤️Heal · 25 manaRestores up to 20 health without exceeding maximum health. Heal targets only the caster and cannot miss.
☠️Poison · 25 manaDeals 5 immediate damage. Poison then deals 5 damage per turn for 2 turns, for 10 additional damage if both ticks complete. Recasting adds 2 more turns. Hit range 6 · Max range 12.
🫸Push · 10 manaMoves the opponent 3 distance away without dealing damage. Useful for escaping melee range. Hit range 8 · Max range 16.
🤜Pull · 10 manaMoves the opponent 3 distance closer without dealing damage, helping weapons reach their target. Hit range 8 · Max range 16.

Consumables

🍎AppleRestores 10 health and 20 mana.
🩹BandageRestores 20 health.
✝️Holy WaterImmediately restores 10 health and grants two Holy ticks. Each later Holy tick restores 5 health and 5 mana.
🥩SteakRestores 5 health, 20 stamina and 5 mana.
